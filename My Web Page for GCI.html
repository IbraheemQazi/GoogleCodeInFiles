Dodge Challenger - The King of all Muscle Cars

A brief History:

The production of the legendary challenger started way back in the 1970s. Many different versions like a convertible and coupe were available and the prices were also fairly low hovering just above $3000. New models came every year with the horse power increasing rapidly. Compare that the first models had about only 100 hp in $3000 while its successors had more than 400 hp in almost the same price. In 1974, the production seized and was continued in 1978 by Mitsubitshi Motors. They kept producing new models till 1983. After that only in 2008, was the Challenger SRT8 was launched. It was the base for the current shape and performance of the Challenger. The 2008 SRT8 was the best of its time. Want to know more about the Dodge Challenger's history? Today's Challengers

These days the Challenger is in its third generation (2008 - now). It has a really cool shape available as a coupe and sedan. The new models are mounted with a powerful super-charged 6.2ltrs V8 engines with All Wheel Drive also available.The prices range from about $30,000 to $50,000 depending on the version and options. The best version, the Hellcat Red-Eye, is the most powerful muscle car on the road today with upto 797 horsepower and 707 lb-ft of torque!
